Cheese Strawberry

Description

These cream cheese strawberry Danish, with perfect, flaky Danish pastry, strawberries, jam and cream cheese are easier than you may think. The perfect Danish recipe from brunch to afternoon tea.

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry
  • Cream Cheese
  • White Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg
  • Water

Directions

  1. Beat the cream cheese. With an electric mixer, blend the cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth
  2. Prepare the puff pastry. Lay one of the puff pastry sheets on the sheet pan. Use a knife to cut strips on each side of the pastry sheet, leaving the middle one completely intact.
  3. Add the filling. Add half of the cream cheese mixture to the center of the pastry sheet, smoothing it out to cover the entire section. Top with the strawberry jam.
  4. Braid the pastry. Fold the top slits of the pastry sheets and cross them into a wide V over the filling. Continue with the rest of the strips, creating a braid-like pattern.
  5. Bake. Coat the top of the danish with milk and sprinkle with the turbinado sugar. Bake at 375F for 25 to 30 minutes, until the top turns golden brown.
  6. Add the glaze. Let the danish cool for 15 minutes then add the glaze. Stir the powdered sugar and milk to create a pourable consistency and drizzle over the top of the danish.
